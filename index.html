🏃‍♂️ HOW TO PLAY & MOVE

🛡️ SHIELD ALERT:
In level 2 and 3, the Sugar Cube has an active shield! You MUST collect the Apple first to break its immunity, or your attacks will fail!
  1. AIM: Press and drag the white Virtual Joystick on the bottom-left to point your hero in a direction.
  2. MOVE: While holding your aim direction, physically walk or jog on the spot to make your character run forward.
  3. STICKY MUD: Avoid the purple puddles in the center! Stepping in them makes you sticky and slows your speed.
  4. SMASH: Follow the glowing cyan radar arrow to find the Apple, grab it, run to the Sugar Cube, and shake your phone firmly to damage it!
⚠️ Safety First:

Check your real-world surroundings! Ensure your floor is flat and free of objects that can trip you.
