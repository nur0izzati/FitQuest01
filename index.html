🏃‍♂️ How To Play

MISSION TARGET:
Find and attack the Sugar Cube Boss to destroy the excess sugar calories!
  • Step 1 (Aim): Hold and drag the virtual joystick on the screen to aim your character in a direction. (Note: Joystick alone will not move you!)
  • Step 2 (Move): While holding the joystick, physically **walk or jog on the spot** to make your character run forward.
  • Step 3 (Attack): Follow the navigation arrow to find the Boss, get close, and firmly **shake your phone** to strike!
⚠️ Safety Warning:

Please make sure your real-world surroundings are entirely clear. DO NOT play near roads, steps, or tight spaces that could cause you to trip or fall!
